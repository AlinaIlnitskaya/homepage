EXPERIENCE

  • 2011

    Del Rey uploaded self-made music videos for her songs "Video Games" and "Blue Jeans" to YouTube, featuring vintage footage interspersed with shots of her singing on her webcam.

  • 2012

    Born to Die was released worldwide, and reached number one in 11 countries, though critical reaction was divided.

  • 2013

    Del Rey filmed Tropico, a musical short film paired to tracks from Paradise, directed by Anthony Mandler. Del Rey screened the film on December 4, 2013 at the Cinerama Dome in Hollywood.

  • 2014

    Del Rey released a cover of "Once Upon a Dream" for the 2014 dark fantasy film Maleficent.

  • 2015

    Del Rey executive produced a short film Hi, How Are You Daniel Johnston, documenting the life of singer-songwriter Daniel Johnston.

  • 2016

    Del Rey premiered a music video for the song "Freak" from Honeymoon at the Wiltern Theatre in Los Angeles.

  • 2017

    Del Rey's fifth studio album, Lust for Life, was released.

  • 2019

    Del Rey presented filmmaker Guillermo del Toro with his star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, and subsequently released a cover of "Season of the Witch" for his film, Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark.

  • 2020

    Del Rey will self-publish a book of poetry Violet Bent Backwards Over The Grass and release a corresponding spoken word album.
